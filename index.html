Web Draw

An online drawing app with a unique control mechanism.

Facebook Slideshow

An automated and customizable online slideshow consisting of pictures fetched via facebook.

Uses facebook APIs to allow users to select a group of friends and pull in their pictures. It then scans facebook tag information to show photos common to the group. Makes use of HTML/Javascript, Facebook Graph API, Facebook Query Language, and PHP.
